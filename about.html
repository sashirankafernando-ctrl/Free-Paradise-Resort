Our Story

The Paradise of Taste & Comfort

Free Paradise Resort was born out of a passion for fresh flavours, genuine warmth, and a cosy atmosphere. Located in the heart of Minuwangoda, Sri Lanka, we envisioned a sanctuary where luxury seamlessly blends with nature.

A heavenly invitation to taste and tranquillity.
